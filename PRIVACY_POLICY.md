# Privacy Policy

## 1. Data Collection

We believe in your right to privacy. This extension **does not collect**:

- Article contents or text.
- Browsing history or URLs visited.
- Personal identifiable information (PII).

## 2. Data Storage

All data related to "saved items" is **stored locally** on your device using the browser's local storage or indexedDB.

- No data is transmitted to our servers.
- No data is shared with third parties.

## 3. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

## 4. Contact

If you have any questions, please open an issue in this GitHub repository.
